CONCRETE POLISHING

MAINE CONCRETE POLISHING SERVICES

Are you ready to enhance your dull or cracked concrete flooring into a low maintenance, durable, and versatile solution?

Look no further than concrete polishing.

Commercial, industrial, and residential spaces can all benefit from professional concrete polishing. Not only will you enjoy a natural, refined look, but you’ll love a cost effective floor that’s easy to clean and will stand the test of time.

If you want to take your existing concrete floor up a notch, consider concrete polishing services.

WHAT MAKES CONCRETE POLISHING IDEAL FOR YOUR HOME OR BUSINESS?

No matter what floor needs updating — your warehouse, your sales floor, or the garage in your home — you want a dependable solution that will last for years to come. That’s exactly what you’ll get with concrete polishing.

The process of concrete polishing uses diamond blades to grind down your existing concrete floor to the desired smooth, polished finish. Polished concrete floors are resistant to stains, scratches, and erosion, and also make cleaning and maintenance simple.

CONCRETE REPAIR & FINISHING

Concrete is amongst one of the most versatile flooring materials available, but it does suffer from its fair share of potential damage. That’s where we come in.

In addition to concrete polishing services, New England Epoxy & Concrete Polishing specializes in concrete repair and finishing.

CONCRETE REPAIR

Prior to sealing, polishing, or finishing, it’s common for concrete to acquire stains from spills or dirt buildup. Cracks and breaks can also occur from heavy weight or machinery, as well as hard impact. Concrete flooring can also be subject to crumbling from old age or hard wear.

One of the most vital steps we take at New England Epoxy & Concrete Polishing is to repair any concrete issues your floor is having prior to polishing. From discoloration to breaks, gaps, and cracks to crumbling concrete that is causing safety issues, our priority is bringing your flooring back (and better) than new.

CONCRETE FINISHING

Depending on your needs, you can choose from a number of different finishes — final coatings or colors — on your concrete flooring.

You may choose a unique color for your garage to set it apart, or you might want an anti-slip or textured coating for your industrial space. Meanwhile, industrial spaces may need exposed aggregate for safety specifications.
